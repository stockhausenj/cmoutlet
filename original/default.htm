Home

Carpet Mill Outlet is family owned and operated and has served the Milwaukee area for over 25 years. We carry the highest quality flooring with some of the best prices in town! Our knowledgeable sales staff offers friendly customer service and is here to help answer all your flooring questions. Located on HWY 100 (between Greenfield Avenue and I-94), our showroom has a HUGE in-stock selection as well as many samples to choose from.

Jim Hishmeh started Carpet Mill Outlet in 1986 and has over 40 years of flooring experience. His visionary skills help customers find the right carpeting for their project. Jim's leadership built a great team here at Carpet Mill Outlet.

Installation

Need help with installation? Don’t sweat it! We employ our own installation team which means you will never have to deal with the subcontractor question mark.

We have a satellite facility in Minocqua. Located just North of Rhinelander, this facility gives us installation access to the entire state. Remember, we install anywhere in Wisconsin!
